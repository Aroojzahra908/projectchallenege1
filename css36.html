Women Empowerments

Women empowerment refers to making women powerful to make them capable of deciding for themselves.

Women have suffered a lot through the years at the hands of men. In earlier centuries, they were treated as
almost non-existent. As if all the rights belonged to men even something as basic as voting. As the times evolved,
women d their power. There on began the revolution for women empowerment.As women were not allowed to
make decisions for them, women empowerment came in like a breath of fresh air. It made them aware of their
rights and how they must make their own place in society rather than depending on a man. It recognized the
fact that things cannot simply work in someones favor because of their gender. However, we still have a long
way to go when we talk about the reasons why we need it.
